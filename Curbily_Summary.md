# Curbily: The Autonomous Operating System for Vertical Entertainment

## Executive Summary
Curbily is a high-performance, AI-driven production suite designed specifically for the rapidly growing $10B vertical drama and micro-content industry. By automating the logistical "heavy lifting"—from script breakdowns to talent sourcing and payments—Curbily allows creators and studios to shift from a traditional 3-month pre-production cycle to 24-hour turnaround scale.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Autonomous Agent Engine
Curbily utilizes specialized AI agents to handle the technical logistics of production:
- **Instant Script Breakdown:** Deciphers 9:16 scripts into line items, cast requirements, and prop lists in seconds.
- **Auto-Scheduling:** Dynamically generates production calendars and call sheets optimized for high-frequency shooting.
- **Financial Architecture:** Real-time budget tracking and automated daily settlements for cast and crew.

### 2. The Talent Network (Actors, Crew, Vendors)
A verified marketplace of production professionals specialized in vertical content:
- **AI Matching:** Producers don't search;they are matched with talent based on project specs, gear, and real-time availability.
- **Identity Decks:** Professional talent profiles (Actors, Crew, Vendors) with verified credits and standardized day rates.
- **Autonomous Booking:** Contracts and NDAs are generated and signed digitally upon booking confirmation.

### 3. The Production Hub
A centralized "Mission Control" for studios to oversee multiple series simultaneously.
- **Visual Progress Tracking:** "Sequence Live" status updates for every scene.
- **Collaborative Suite:** Cross-functional access for directors, producers, and agents to coordinate in real-time.
