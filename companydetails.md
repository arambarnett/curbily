# Curbily: Company Details, GTM Strategy, & Content Guidelines

This document provides context for generating marketing materials, LinkedIn outreach, articles, and newsletters for **Curbily**—the world's first AI-powered "Head of Production in a Box."

## 1. Company Overview

*   **Name:** Curbily
*   **Tagline:** Your Head of Production in a Box.
*   **Mission:** To eliminate the manual friction of pre-production, allowing filmmakers and creators to spend time shaping the creative, not filling out spreadsheets.
*   **The Problem:** Traditional hollywood software (StudioBinder, Movie Magic) are just digital filing cabinets. A producer still has to manually read the script, highlight props, build the schedule, and calculate budgets. This takes weeks and thousands of dollars.
*   **The Solution:** Curbily ingests a script/concept and autonomously generates an exhaustive line-by-line breakdown, schedule (DOOD), top-sheet budget, and call sheets. Our AI agents can even do automated email/SMS/voice outreach to crew and venues to check availability and negotiate rates.
*   **Value Prop:** Save weeks of pre-pro time. Enable indie producers to handle larger slates. Enable digital creators (top 1% YouTubers) to run mini-studios without hiring expensive Hollywood overhead.

## 2. Target Audiences & Outreach Angles

When generating outreach messages for LinkedIn, target the following personas with these specific angles:

**A. Line Producers / UPMs (Traditional Film/Commercial)**
*   **Brainset:** Overworked, stressed, drowning in Excel and Movie Magic.
*   **Angle:** "What if you could generate a complete 1st-pass breakdown and top-sheet in 60 seconds instead of 2 weeks? Curbily doesn't replace you; it makes you a superhuman who can oversee 3x the projects and make more money. Step out of the manual data entry."

**B. Directors / Indie Filmmakers**
*   **Brainset:** Wants to focus on the creative vision but gets bogged down by logistical realities and budget constraints.
*   **Angle:** "Focus on the screen, let AI handle the logistics. Curbily generates your schedules, budgets, and call sheets so you don't have to hire a $3k/week line producer just to get your project off the ground."

**C. Talent Managers & Digital Creators (New Hollywood)**
*   **Brainset:** Their clients are scaling from 10-minute vlog videos to 60-minute high-production features. They need Hollywood-level logistics but refuse to learn clunky legacy software.
*   **Angle:** "Turn your top creators into autonomous mini-studios. Curbily gives you a Head of Production in a Box. Scale ambition without inflating payroll overhead."

## 3. Brand Voices & "Propaganda" Guidelines

The goal is to flood the zone with positive propaganda, creating FOMO and positioning Curbily as the inevitable future of production. The message is: *"Adapt and thrive, or stick to spreadsheets and get left behind."* 

We will communicate across three distinct voices:

### Voice 1: Jon's LinkedIn (The Visionary / Disruptor)
*   **Style:** Aggressive, visionary, energetic, builder-focused. Uses short, punchy sentences. High conviction. 
*   **Themes:** The future of the Creator Economy, why legacy software is dead, macro-shifts in media, building products that actually solve problems.
*   **Example hook:** "Movie Magic was built for the 90s. We just built the software that will replace it."

### Voice 2: Aram's LinkedIn (The Pragmatist / Operator)
*   **Style:** Insightful, operational, tactical. Focuses on the "how" and the precise mechanics of efficiency.
*   **Themes:** Product execution, AI agent workflows, fixing broken physical processes, the math behind production savings. 
*   **Example hook:** "I spent the weekend analyzing how many hours a Line Producer spends just highlighting props in a script. It's insane. Here's how we automated it down to 45 seconds."

### Voice 3: Curbily Brand / Blog (The Authority)
*   **Style:** Professional, authoritative, industry-insider, sleek. 
*   **Themes:** Product updates, case studies, "The Future of Production", actionable guides for filmmakers.
*   **Example hook:** "How the new wave of Digital Creators are adopting Studio-level logistics (without the overhead)."

## 4. Newsletter Strategy: "The Daily/Weekly Call Sheet"

*   **Goal:** Build a recurring audience of producers, filmmakers, and creators.
*   **Cadence:** Weekly.
*   **Format:**
    1.  **The Opening Slate:** A quick, punchy thought on the state of the industry (The Digital Shift).
    2.  **On-Set Tactics:** One practical tip for running a better physical production (often tying back to how Curbily automates it).
    3.  **The Hot Sheet:** Who is building / filming what (creates FOMO—"Look at all these people using Curbily to get greenlit").
    4.  **Feature Spotlight:** A GIF or quick breakdown of a new Curbily feature.
