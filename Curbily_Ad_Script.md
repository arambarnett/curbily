# Curbily 30-Second Ad Script: "The Future of Production"

**Objective:** Get Cast, Crew, and Vendors to sign up for the Curbily Talent Network.
**Vibe:** Brutalist, Fast-paced, Tech-forward, High-energy.

---

### [0:00 - 0:05] THE HOOK
**Visual:** 
- Fast rhythmic cuts of a 9:16 vertical drama scene (high tension). 
- Glitch effect overlay.
- Text on screen: **THE $10B INDUSTRY.**

**Audio:** 
- Intense, driving synth bass. 
- *Sound Effect (SFX):* Cinematic "Whoosh" + Camera shutter.

**Voiceover (VO):** 
"The vertical drama gold rush is here. But the bottleneck? Production logistics."

---

### [0:05 - 0:15] THE PROBLEM & SOLUTION
**Visual:** 
- Split screen showing messy spreadsheets vs. a clean, brutalist Curbily dashboard.
- Icons of **ACTORS**, **CREW**, and **VENDORS** pop in rhythm with the music.

**VO:** 
"No more messy threads. No more chasing payments. Curbily is the world’s first autonomous production suite designed for 9:16 dominance."

---

### [0:15 - 0:25] THE NETWORK
**Visual:** 
- Rapid-fire profile cards of diverse talent (DPs, Stunt Leads, Actors).
- Text on screen: **MATCHED BY AI. BOOKED IN SECONDS.**

**VO:** 
"Enter our elite network. We match your specific skills to real projects with real budgets—instantly."

---

### [0:25 - 0:30] THE CALL TO ACTION (CTA)
**Visual:** 
- Curbily Logo (Black & Blue).
- Screen Recording: Hand tapping "JOIN THE NETWORK" on a mobile device.
- URL on screen: **CURBILY.COM/JOIN**

**VO:** 
"Stop waiting. Start booking. Curbily: Work in the future of entertainment. Sign up now."

**Audio:** 
- Final heavy synth hit. 
- *SFX:* Digital "Ping."

---

## Instructions for Creators:
1.  **Format:** Shoot 9:16 (Vertical).
2.  **Edit:** Use "Jump-cuts" every 0.5 to 1 second to keep energy high.
3.  **Typography:** Use bold, uppercase sans-serif fonts (like Inter or Space Grotesk) with high contrast background.
4.  **Mood:** Make it feel industrial and inevitable.
